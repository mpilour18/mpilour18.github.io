About

All about therapy.

  • What is therapy?

    Therapy, also called counseling, is the process of meeting with a therapist to resolve problematic behaviors, beliefs, feelings, relationships and somatic responses

    -Community mental health.
    -School and career.

    -Helps you learn life-long coping skills.
    -Changes how you interact with people in your life.
    -Can make you feel happier.
    -Through its link to happiness, it leads to more productivity.
    -Helps improve chronic stress.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive behavioral therapy is a short term therapy technique that can help people find new ways to behave by changing their thoughts pattern.It focuses on exploring and developing methods to deal with challenges and behaviors that arise day to day.It can be effective in treating depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and other mental conditions.

  • Name: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Contraction: CBT

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

Dialectical Behavioral Therapy helps people tolerate and regulate their emotions and improve their relationships with others.It's based on cognitive behavioral principles and focuses on problem solving and acceptance.It can be effective in treating ADHD, Major Depressive Disorder, Substance Use Disorder and Suicidal Behavior.

  • Name: Dialectical Behaviour Therapy
  • Contraction: DBT

Exposure Therapy

Exposure Therapy is a form of behavioral therapy designed to help you face your fears.When you're scared of a specific object or activity, you may avoid it.Exposure Therapy can help break this cycle of fear and avoidance.It's effective in treaating phobias, panic disorders and social anxiety disorder.

  • Name: Exposure Therapy
  • Contraction: ET

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing Therapy is a psychotherapy that uses rhythmic left-right(bilateral) stimulation to help people recover from trauma or other distressing life experiences.It's effective in treating additions, anxiety, chronic pain and eating disorders.

  • Name: Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing Therapy
  • Contraction: EMDR

Interpersonal Therapy

Interpersonal Therapy is a short term form of psychotherapy, that is used to treat depression and other conditions.As its name suggests, IPT focuses on your interpersonal relationships and social interactions.It's effective in treating BPD, Dysthymia, Perinatal and Postpartum depression.

  • Name: Interpersonal Therapy
  • Contraction: IPT

Group Therapy

Group Therapy is a form of psychotherapy that aims to help people manage mental health conditions or cope with negative experiences and behaviors.It involves one or more mental health practitioners who deliver psychotherapy to several individuals in each session.It's effective in treating post-traumatic stress disorder, grief, domestic violence and weight loss.

  • Name: Group Therapy
  • Contraction: GT
